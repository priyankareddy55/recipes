Ingredients:
 - Milk (as per requirement)
 - Sugar (as per requirement)
 - Tea powder (one spoon per one cup milk)
 - Ginger (a small piece)
 - elaichi (half spoon/2-3 pieces)

Directions:
1. Pour the milk into a pan and boil them till warm on the stove.
2. Then add the tea powder and ginger as per the requirement and how strong one prefers their tea.
3. Let the tea mixture boil very well till we obtain a strong color and aroma. Then add sugar and elaichi and let it boil for a few more minutes.
4. After done, por the tea into tea cups and add some mint leaves for flavour and smell.
